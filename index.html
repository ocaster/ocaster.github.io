About

Us




Cam Digital Creations was founded by CEO Camilo Sanchez in 2013. In a matter of two years, CDC was able to gain momentum and bring several companies and professionals the digital design services they required; from managing the development and design of business cards, reconstructing professional logos to creating web overlays and banners.

"From art class to design graduate, to freelance and business owner, my passion towards technology and the digital world brought me into this field which I am proudly working towards sharing with the rest of the GTA."

As of January 2016, CDC now reaches beyond the professional market and into your homes. "We are able to assist with photo re-touching, poster development, personal shirt design and much more. My team and I rarely turn away an opportunity to demonstrate our skills, knowledge and creativity to bring your ideas to life."
